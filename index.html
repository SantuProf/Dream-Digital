About

What you think about us ?

The Dream Digital Computer Training Centres run by the Department of NYCTA (National Youth Computer Training Academy) play an important role in providing low cost computer training to the student-youth across the state enabling them to prepare themselves fit for employment. About 1,000 (One Thousand) Youth Computer Training centres are now running across the state. Every year thousands of student-youth are getting themselves eligible to survive in the competitive job market successfully being trained from these training centres in various courses.
